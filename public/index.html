Clipper 2.0 Savings Calculator

In December, the Bay Area's transit card is getting an upgrade.

Instead of being penalized when changing from a bus to a train, or from one agency to another, Clipper 2.0 comes with free transfers. This means that no matter where you're going in the region, there's no need to worry about getting overcharged.

Interested in finding out how much you can save on your trips? Use this trip calculator to find out how much you can save in transfers starting December 10th.
